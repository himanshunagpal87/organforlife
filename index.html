Our Mission

Fighting the Organ Shortage Crisis

The OrganForLife (OFL) Foundation is a grassroots non-profit working to close the catastrophic gap between organ supply and demand in India — through youth-driven awareness.

⚖️

Legal & Safe

Under the Transplantation of Human Organs Act, 1994, organ donation is fully regulated. The sale of organs is a crime — voluntary donation is the only ethical path to saving lives.

🤝

No Cost to Your Family

There is no cost to a donor's family for the donation itself. Transplant-related expenses are borne by the recipient and the medical system.

🙏

Supported by All Faiths

Every major religion in India supports or permits organ donation. Giving the gift of life is a universally honoured act of compassion.

How It Works

Three Simple Steps

Becoming an organ donor is simpler than most people think. Here's how you leave your legacy.

1
📝

Register Your Pledge

Fill out the organ-donation pledge form and specify which organs you wish to donate. Your decision is recorded securely in the national registry.

2
💬

Inform Your Family

Share your decision with your next of kin. Family consent is essential at the time of donation, so make sure your loved ones know and honour your wish.

3
🌱

Spread Awareness

Share your pledge on social media and encourage friends and community to register. Every conversation you start could save another life.

What You Can Donate

Organs & Tissues

A single person can donate multiple organs and tissues — giving the gift of life to many recipients.

❤️

Heart

Saves the life of someone with end-stage heart failure.

🫁

Lungs

Help patients with pulmonary disease and cystic fibrosis breathe again.

🧡

Liver

Can be split to help two recipients simultaneously.

🫘

Kidneys

The most-needed organ — two per donor means two lives saved.

🩹

Pancreas

Critical for patients living with Type 1 diabetes.

👁️

Eyes / Corneas

Restore sight to those living with corneal blindness.

🦴

Bone & Tissue

Used in reconstructive surgery and healing severe injuries.

🩸

Blood & Platelets

Living donation — saves lives every single day.
